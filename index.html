Dr. Mina is one of the few board certified orthodontists in San Diego area. She has been practicing dentistry since 2009 and orthodontics since 2017. She has treated many kids and adults with complex orthodontic conditions such as cleft lip and palate, mal-alignmnets of the jaws requiring orthognathic surgery, impacted teeth, airway deficiencies and individuals with missing teeth combined with restorative dental treatments. She has extensive experience with clear aligners and other fixed and removable orthodontic appliances. It is for these reasons we are elated to have her as our Poway Orthodontist!
